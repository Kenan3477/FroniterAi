# Phase 3 AI Features - Agent Training Guide

## 🚀 OMNIVOX AI PHASE 3: ADVANCED DIALLER FEATURES

Welcome to the future of AI-powered calling! Phase 3 introduces cutting-edge artificial intelligence to enhance your productivity and success rates.

### 🎯 NEW AI FEATURES OVERVIEW

#### 1. Real-time Sentiment Analysis 📊
**What it does:** Analyzes customer speech and tone in real-time during calls
**How it helps you:**
- See instant mood indicators (😊 Positive, 😐 Neutral, 😕 Negative)
- Receive coaching prompts based on customer sentiment
- Adjust your approach mid-call for better outcomes

**How to use:**
1. During calls, check the Sentiment Panel on your right
2. Watch for color-coded indicators:
   - 🟢 Green: Customer is receptive, continue current approach
   - 🟡 Yellow: Customer is neutral, try to engage more
   - 🔴 Red: Customer is frustrated, use empathy and de-escalation
3. Follow the AI coaching suggestions that appear

#### 2. AI-Powered Auto-Disposition 🤖
**What it does:** Suggests the best call outcome based on conversation analysis
**How it helps you:**
- Reduce time spent choosing dispositions
- Improve accuracy of call outcomes
- Get consistent disposition choices across your team

**How to use:**
1. After each call, the AI Disposition Panel appears
2. Review the recommended disposition with confidence score
3. Accept the suggestion or choose a different option
4. The system learns from your choices to improve recommendations

#### 3. AI-Driven Lead Scoring 🎯
**What it does:** Ranks your contacts by likelihood to convert
**How it helps you:**
- Focus on high-potential leads first
- Maximize your conversion rates
- Spend time on the most promising prospects

**How to use:**
1. Check your Contact Queue for priority rankings
2. Look for star ratings (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ = Highest priority)
3. Call high-scored leads during peak hours
4. Use suggested talking points for each lead

#### 4. Real-time Dial Rate Management ⚡
**What it does:** Automatically adjusts calling pace based on agent availability
**How it helps you:**
- Reduces wait times between calls
- Minimizes dropped calls
- Optimizes your talk time

**How to use:**
1. Monitor the Dial Rate Dashboard
2. See current pace and predictions
3. System automatically adjusts - no action needed
4. Focus on conversations, not timing

### 💡 BEST PRACTICES FOR AI FEATURES

#### Sentiment Analysis Tips
✅ **DO:**
- Glance at sentiment indicators every 30 seconds
- Follow AI coaching prompts for difficult conversations
- Use positive sentiment to push for closes
- Document sentiment patterns for different contact types

❌ **DON'T:**
- Stare at the screen during conversations
- Ignore negative sentiment warnings
- Assume sentiment is always 100% accurate
- Rely solely on AI without using your judgment

#### Auto-Disposition Best Practices
✅ **DO:**
- Read the AI reasoning before accepting suggestions
- Provide feedback when you disagree with recommendations
- Use custom notes to add context the AI might miss
- Trust the system for routine dispositions

❌ **DON'T:**
- Accept all recommendations without review
- Skip adding notes for complex situations
- Ignore low confidence scores (< 80%)
- Use auto-disposition for sensitive calls

#### Lead Scoring Optimization
✅ **DO:**
- Prioritize 4-5 star leads during peak calling hours
- Use AI talking points as conversation starters
- Update contact information when scores seem incorrect
- Track conversion rates by lead score

❌ **DON'T:**
- Only call high-scored leads (maintain variety)
- Ignore lower-scored leads completely
- Assume high scores guarantee success
- Skip building rapport because of high scores

### 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ISSUES

#### Sentiment Analysis Not Showing
1. Check your microphone permissions
2. Ensure call recording is enabled
3. Verify internet connection stability
4. Contact IT if issues persist

#### Auto-Disposition Suggestions Incorrect
1. Provide feedback using the thumbs up/down buttons
2. Add detailed notes explaining the correct outcome
3. The AI will learn from your corrections
4. Report patterns to your supervisor

#### Lead Scores Seem Wrong
1. Update contact information if outdated
2. Check for data entry errors
3. Review recent interaction history
4. Request scoring recalibration if needed

#### Dial Rate Too Fast/Slow
1. Check your availability status
2. Verify campaign settings are correct
3. Monitor system load indicators
4. Adjust personal pace preferences in settings
